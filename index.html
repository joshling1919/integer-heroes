Welcome to Integer-Heroes!



This is a game that gives students a different way of understanding the addition of integers.
To get started, click on the arrow to the RIGHT and read through the tips!
Tip #1: Click 'Submit' or hit 'Enter' when finished with a step.
Tip #2: Directions for each step are at the top!
Tip #3: Each superhero represents positive one. Each villain represent negative one.
Tip #4: To correctly represent an integer, choose the correct number of heroes or villains.
Tip #5: If they're from different sides (ie. heroes vs. villains), they fight! Otherwise, they join forces.
